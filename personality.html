Works in team


Protagonist Subordinates With the ability to learn quickly and multitask excellently, people with the protagonist personality type are able to take on multiple responsibilities with competence and good drive. This is because some managers will take advantage of the protagonist's excellent character by making excessive demands and using extra work to burden their protagonist's subordinates.
